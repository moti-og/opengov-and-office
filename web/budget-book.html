Fund Description

The General Fund is the City's major operating fund. All revenues which, by law or for administrative control, are not in separate funds are deposited in the General Fund. The General Fund is used to provide: police and fire services; planning, engineering and building inspection; recreation and leisure programs; and overall City administration, including management, finance and treasury, clerk's office, human resources, and property maintenance.
